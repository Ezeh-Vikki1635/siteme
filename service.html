Professional Services

We offer broad range of services in the specialized field of Real Estate Practice as follows:

  1. Development appraisal entailing preparation of feasibility and viability reports on proposed site and packaging of finance for development.
  2. Property Agency Leasing, sale and acquisition of real estate for and on behalf of clients.
  3. Property management comprising lease, management of leases and services Administration.
  4. Property management involving administration of activities on construction or real estate.
  5. Real Estate Consultant Services including specialized area of facility management of projects.

Our management activities include:

  • Appraisal of subject property aimed at determining management techniques to be employed.
  • Determination of the nature and design of each of the service facilities to ascertain their use intensity.
  • Negotiation of economic lease based on our careful analysis of the market.
  • Carefully selection of tenant when property is vacant.
  • Collection of rent.
  • Detailed inventory of the fixtures and fittings, plant and machinery, service installations on the premises.
  • Preparation of specific lease agreement and execution of same before handing over of premises to tenants(s).
  • Enforcement of such tenant’s obligations to payment of electricity, water bills, telephone bills, waste disposal and other municipal charges.
  • Regular inspection of properties to forestall improper use, alterations and conversions.
  • General Real Estate Consultancy aimed at achieving “highest and best use” on management properties.
